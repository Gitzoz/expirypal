Privacy summary

Local-only by design.

ExpiryPal is built around a narrow promise: keep data on device, avoid tracking, and keep notifications local.

App guarantees

  • Food item data is stored locally on device.
  • No account system is required.
  • No cloud sync or backend infrastructure is used.
  • Notifications are local notifications only.

What ExpiryPal does not use

  • Tracking
  • Analytics
  • Ads
  • Third-party SDKs

Public site constraints

  • No analytics or tracking pixels
  • No cookies or third-party embeds
  • No remote assets or external scripts
  • Informational only, with no form handling

For the authoritative repository statement, read the privacy document in the repository.
