Product promise

Built to stay small, understandable, and local.

Reduce food waste

Add what you bought, see what expires soon, and keep urgency visible on one dashboard.

Stay private

Data stays on device, reminders are local only, and the project does not use tracking, analytics, or third-party SDKs.

Keep it simple

ExpiryPal is intentionally narrow: a paid app with a clear v1 scope, local-only data, and no speculative features.

How it works

A short path from item entry to timely reminders.

01

Add items

Capture a name, expiry date, storage location, and optional details.

02

See urgency

Dashboard groups active items into Today, Next 3 Days, and Later.

03

Get local reminders

Notifications are local-only reminders at 3 days, 1 day, and expiry day, controlled from Settings.

Privacy by design

Repository, app, and public site all follow the same rule set.

Local-only data

Food item data is stored on device with SwiftData. There is no backend.

No tracking

The app and this site avoid analytics, tracking pixels, ads, and third-party embeds.

Clear licensing

Source code is MIT-licensed. ExpiryPal branding remains proprietary.
