# Privacy

ExpiryPal stores user data locally on device.

This repository may also publish a public GitHub Pages site that describes the product and links to the documentation. That site is informational only and must not include tracking, analytics, ads, cookies, or third-party embeds.

ExpiryPal does not use:
- tracking
- analytics
- ads
- backend services
- third-party SDKs

Notifications are local notifications only.

Public website summary:
- product information only
- no account system
- no form submission or backend processing
- no analytics or tracking pixels
